- Unordered lists: <ul> and <li>

- Inline quotes: <q>
### Lesson 6: Tables
- Tables: <table>

- Forms: <form>, <input>, <label>, <textarea>, <select>, <option>, <button>

- Grouping with <div> and inline grouping with <span>
