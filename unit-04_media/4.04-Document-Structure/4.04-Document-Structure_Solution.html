Today's Headlines

City Council Approves New Library Funding

In a unanimous vote last night, the city council approved $2.3 million in funding for the new downtown library branch. Construction is expected to begin in spring 2025.

Mayor Jennifer Martinez praised the decision, calling it "an investment in our community's future and a commitment to literacy for all residents."

Local High School Wins State Championship

Washington State School for the Blind's debate team captured the state championship for the third consecutive year. The team defeated Lincoln High in the final round with their compelling argument on environmental policy.

Coach Sarah Thompson credited the students' hard work and dedication throughout the season.
