Amazing Space Photography

Space exploration has given us incredible images that help us understand our universe. Here are some of my favorite space photographs that show the beauty and wonder of the cosmos.

The Pillars of Creation

This famous image from the Hubble Space Telescope shows star-forming regions in the Eagle Nebula. The towering columns are made of gas and dust where new stars are being born.

Earth from Space

This "Blue Marble" photograph was taken by the Apollo 17 crew in 1972. It shows our entire planet as a beautiful blue sphere floating in the darkness of space.

Saturn's Rings

Saturn's ring system is one of the most beautiful sights in our solar system. This image from the Cassini spacecraft shows the planet during equinox when the rings cast dramatic shadows.

The International Space Station

The International Space Station orbits Earth every 90 minutes. This image shows the station's solar panels and laboratory modules where astronauts live and work in space.

A Galaxy Far, Far Away

The Pinwheel Galaxy is located 21 million light-years from Earth. Its spiral arms contain billions of stars and show us what our own Milky Way galaxy might look like from the outside.
