My Journey Learning Languages

Why I Love Languages

Learning languages has opened up so many opportunities for me. I can communicate with people from different cultures and understand movies and books in their original language. It's challenging but incredibly rewarding.

Aprender idiomas ha abierto muchas oportunidades para mí. Puedo comunicarme con personas de diferentes culturas y entender películas y libros en su idioma original. Es desafiante pero increíblemente gratificante.

Apprendre les langues m'a ouvert de nombreuses opportunités. Je peux communiquer avec des personnes de différentes cultures et comprendre les films et les livres dans leur langue d'origine. C'est difficile mais incroyablement gratifiant.

学习语言为我打开了许多机会。我可以与来自不同文化的人交流，并用原始语言理解电影和书籍。这很有挑战性，但也非常有益。

Daily Practice

Every morning I practice vocabulary. I might learn that biblioteca means library, or that parapluie means umbrella. These small words add up to big progress over time.

My goal is to be conversational in all three languages by next year!
