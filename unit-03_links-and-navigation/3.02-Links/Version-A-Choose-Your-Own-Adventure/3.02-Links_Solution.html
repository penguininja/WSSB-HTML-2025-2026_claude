The Mysterious School Locker

You're walking down the hallway when you notice an old, forgotten locker that's slightly open. A strange blue light is coming from inside. What do you do?

Do you investigate the mysterious locker or ignore it and keep walking?

You Investigate the Locker

You slowly open the locker door wider. Inside, you find a glowing crystal and an old notebook filled with strange symbols. The crystal feels warm to the touch.

Suddenly, you hear footsteps approaching. Do you quickly hide behind the locker or stand your ground and see who's coming?

You Ignore the Locker

You decide it's none of your business and keep walking to class. But as you reach the end of the hallway, you hear a loud CRASH behind you!

You turn around to see the locker door has slammed shut and the blue light is gone. Do you go back to investigate what happened or hurry to class and pretend you saw nothing?

You Hide Behind the Locker

You duck behind the locker just as your science teacher, Ms. Rodriguez, rounds the corner. She walks straight to the mysterious locker and pulls out a key!

"I knew someone would find it eventually," she mutters, locking the locker securely. She notices you hiding and smiles. "Interested in real science mysteries? Meet me after school in the lab."

Ending: You've discovered that your teacher is involved in something mysterious! Start the adventure over to try a different path.

You Stand Your Ground

A student you don't recognize approaches. They're wearing clothes that look like they're from the 1950s!

"Finally! Someone else can see the locker," they say excitedly. "I've been trapped in this school for 70 years. That crystal is the key to getting me home. Will you help me?"

You Hurry to Class

You decide some mysteries are better left unsolved and rush to chemistry class. But you can't stop thinking about that blue light...

After school, you find a note in your own locker: "Some doors are meant to be opened. -A Friend"
