Welcome to Green Valley Community Center

Green Valley Community Center has served the neighborhood since 1987. We offer programs for all ages, from toddler playgroups to senior fitness classes. Our mission is to bring people together through learning, wellness, and community events.

Our Programs

Youth Arts

Drawing, painting, and sculpture classes for ages 6-17. Held every Saturday from 10am to 12pm in the Arts Wing.

Adult Fitness

Yoga, strength training, and cardio sessions. Morning and evening classes available Monday through Friday.

Senior Social

Weekly gatherings with games, guest speakers, and coffee. Every Wednesday from 1pm to 3pm in the Main Hall.

Tech Help Desk

Free one-on-one help with phones, tablets, and computers. Drop in Tuesdays and Thursdays from 4pm to 6pm.
