Peanut Butter Sandwich Recipe:

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic, and one of my favorite meals. You'll love this simple recipe!

Ingredients:

2 slices of bread
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 tablespoon jelly
Spread and enjoy!

Directions:

To make a peanut butter sandwich, take two slices of bread and spread peanut butter on one of them, and jam on the other. Press them together and enjoy!

For an extra treat, try adding honey, bananas, or cereal!
