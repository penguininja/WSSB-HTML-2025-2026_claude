Recent Trail Discovery

Last weekend, we explored the Hidden Falls Trail - a moderate 3-mile hike that leads to a spectacular waterfall. The green forest canopy and crystal-clear water created perfect photography conditions.

Wildlife Spotting Tips

Early morning hikes offer the best opportunities for wildlife viewing. We've spotted deer, various bird species, and even the occasional fox along our trails.

Safety Information

⚠️ Trail Conditions Alert

Due to recent rainfall, the Pine Ridge Trail is muddy and potentially slippery. Please use extra caution and wear appropriate footwear.

✅ Trail Maintenance Complete

Great news! The Sunset Vista Trail has been cleared of fallen trees and is now fully accessible. Perfect timing for fall foliage viewing!

Essential Hiking Gear

  • Sturdy hiking boots
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Plenty of water (at least 1 liter per person)
  • First aid kit for emergencies
  • Trail map and compass or GPS device

Pro Tip

Always inform someone of your hiking plans and expected return time. This demonstrates good contrast with white text on dark blue background.
