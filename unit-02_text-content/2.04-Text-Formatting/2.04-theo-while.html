Blog Post: My First Concert Experience

Introduction

When I was in high school, I went to my first concert at The Gorge Ampitheater, and it was an unforgettable experience. Beyonce and Buddy Holly opened for Nina Simone and hearing them live gave me chills. I realized how powerful music can be.

The Songs

Beyonce performed songs like Single Ladies and Texas Hold 'Em. I like her new country album a lot.

Buddy Holly sang songs like Everyday and Maybe Baby. The Classics.

Nina Simone played songs like Feeling Good and I Put a Spell on You.

Final Thoughts

Overall, I had an amazing experience and I would defintiely go to a live concert again!
