Photography: My Creative Passion

Essential Photography Equipment

  • Digital camera or smartphone
  • Extra batteries and memory cards
  • Tripod for stable shots
  • Camera lens cleaning kit
  • Camera bag for protection

Steps to Take a Great Photo

  1. Find interesting lighting
  2. Compose your shot using the rule of thirds
  3. Focus on your subject
  4. Take multiple shots from different angles
  5. Review and edit your favorites

Photography Styles I Want to Master

  • Portrait Photography
    • Natural light portraits
    • Studio lighting setups
    • Environmental portraits
  • Landscape Photography
    • Golden hour shots
    • Long exposure techniques
    • Mountain and forest scenes
  • Street Photography
